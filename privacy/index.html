1. Introduction

Welcome to Comictrics ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Comictrics (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.

Important: By using our App, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and profile preferences when you create an account
  • Comic Preferences: Favorite publishers, price ranges, notification settings, and investment preferences
  • Watchlist Data: Comics you save, mark as favorites, or add to your portfolio
  • Feedback: Reviews, ratings, and support communications
  • User-Generated Content: Comments, reviews, or notes you add to comic recommendations

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in app, navigation patterns, tap interactions

  • Analytics Data: App usage patterns, feature engagement, and user flow analytics

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Services: Basic profile information if you sign in through Google, Apple, or other social providers
  • Comic Data Sources: Public comic book information from databases and market sources
  • Analytics Services: Aggregated usage statistics from third-party analytics providers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide daily comic book recommendations and market analysis
  • Personalize recommendations based on your investment preferences and history
  • Send push notifications about daily picks, price alerts, and market trends (with your consent)
  • Track and display your portfolio performance and investment history
  • Improve our recommendation algorithms and market analysis accuracy
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Analyze app usage to enhance user experience and develop new features
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Send important app updates and service announcements
