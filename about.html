About Comictrics

Comictrics (pronounced "Comic-trics") combines Comic + Metrics for analytical collectors.

What is Comictrics?

Comictrics is a Comic Book Metrics application that brings data-driven analytics to comic book collecting. We help collectors make informed decisions about which comics are worth buying, cleaning, pressing, and grading for maximum value.

Think of us as your personal comic book collecting advisor, powered by real market data and expert analysis.

The Name: "Comic-trics"

Comictrics is pronounced "Comic-trics" and combines two key concepts:

  • Comic - Comic books and graphic novels
  • Metrics - Data, analytics, and measurable insights

Just like how "analytics" helps businesses make better decisions, Comic Book Metrics helps collectors make smarter collecting choices in the comic book market.

Our Mission

We believe comic book collecting should be both enjoyable and rewarding. Too many collectors buy comics blindly, hoping they'll increase in value, only to discover they've made poor collecting decisions.

Comictrics changes that by providing:

  • Daily value picks with detailed analysis
  • Market trends and price tracking
  • Grading cost vs. value assessments
  • Data-driven collecting recommendations

How Comic Book Metrics Work

Our analytics engine analyzes thousands of data points including:

  • Historical Prices - Track how values change over time
  • Market Availability - Understand supply and demand
  • Grading Costs - Calculate if grading increases value
  • Condition Assessment - Estimate potential grades
  • Value Potential - Predict future value growth

This data powers our daily recommendations, helping you focus on comics with the highest profit potential.
