Important: This Action Cannot Be Undone

Once your account is deleted, all your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered. Please ensure you've exported any data you wish to keep before proceeding.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • All your comic book collection data
  • Your watchlist and portfolio information
  • Reading history and preferences
  • Custom lists and categories
  • Performance analytics and metrics
  • All app settings and preferences

What May Be Retained

  • Anonymized analytics data for service improvement
  • Transaction records for legal and tax requirements (7 years)
  • Communications required for regulatory compliance
  • Data necessary to prevent fraud or abuse

Deletion Timeline

Within 48 hours: Your account will be deactivated and inaccessible

Within 30 days: All personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems

After 30 days: Deletion confirmation email will be sent to your registered email address
